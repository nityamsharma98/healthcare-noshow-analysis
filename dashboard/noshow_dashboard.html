⚠ ANALYST FLAG — SMS reminder is a confound, not a cause

Raw read: patients who got an SMS appear to miss more often — reminders look harmful.

Why that's wrong: SMS is sent mostly for long-lead appointments (see the average lead days below), which are inherently higher-risk. The lead time drives both. Try filtering to a single lead-time-heavy segment and watch the gap shrink.
